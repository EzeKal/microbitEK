The rpsbit app is a rock paper, scissors app for the micro:bit V2.

How to install on your micro:bit V2:
First, download the rpsbit.py app in this repo.
Then, open 'https://python.microbit.org/'.
After that, click the Open button on the website.
A file picker should open up, select the rpsbit.py file you opened.
Now, press the Save button on the website to save a .hex file to your device.
Finally, plug in the micro:bit and transfer the file to the drive called 'MICROBIT'.
Wait for it to flash.
Now you're done!

How to play:

You play against the micro:bit.

Options:
1. Gold Logo - Rock
2. Button A - Paper
3. Button B - Scissors

To use it, first wait for the 321 countdown to end so it goes back to the happy face, then click the option you want to choose.
After that, the micro:bit will choose it's option by showing an arrow towards the option it has chose.

Finally, it will show one of these symbols with a sound effect:
1. A Tick - This means you have won.
2. A Cross - This means you have lost.
3. A face with closed eyes and a straight mouth. - This means you have a tie, both sides have chose the same option.

Tip: If you want to play another match against the micro:bit V2, you can press the 'Reset' button on the back of your micro:bit V2.
